Hut Meets

We had two hut meets in March:

17-19 March: Hut Maintenance Weekend

8 of us spent the weekend up at the hut and, in between pub visits (the hut kitchen was out of order as it was our work site…well that's our excuse), we managed to get a number of jobs done. The list included re-painting, damp treatment, varnishing and starting to transform the space outside the front of the hut to name just a few of the jobs.

Thanks to everyone who made it!

24-26 March 2017: New Members' Weekend

13 of us made it to the hut. The weather couldn't have been better. Three groups went out on the Saturday: Walkers, Climbers and the third group a combination of walking and scrambling - I believe that an ice axe was even used at one point! Most were up before 7am and the majority of people were out by 8.30am! That must be a first for the Club!

Annelyse cooked an amazing communal meal (no surprise there) that evening.

Unfortunately the members weren't as sprightly the following morning…most of us getting up around 8.30am. However, we made the most of the weather. Some went walking and others of us headed up to the Upper Tier to get some more climbing in. What a great start to the year!

The next meet is 28 to 30 April at the Hut. See you there!
