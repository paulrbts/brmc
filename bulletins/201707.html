June Weekend Meet Review

The Hut

Dave and Paul were at the hut on 16-18 June. It was a scorching weekend and, after a warm walk-in, made their way up Cwm Silyn. It was cold. And windy. Very. After topping out into the heat and stripping layers off, clinging onto all their gear, they made it back to the hut and up Valerie's Rib to finish the day.

Sunday was a scurry up the famed Grooved Arête on Tryfan. Another roasting day, this time in full sun, which made for good alpine prep!

As a Meets Leader all that is required is:

  • Advertising on Facebook and the Forum (NB: Events are already created on the FB page – they just need re–advertising)

  • If at the Hut, ensuring the keys are available for those arriving first

  • Reporting back to Mike the numbers who stayed
  • Ensuring the Log Book is completed
