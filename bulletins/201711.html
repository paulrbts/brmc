New Committee

Each year, the Committee steps down at the AGM and a new Committee voted in How does this work? Members are nominated for roles by other members. On the evening itself, those nominated are put forward for each of the roles they are nominated for and another member is required to ‘second’ that nomination. It then goes to member votes. Whoever has the most number of votes is elected.

We invite each and every one of you to consider putting yourself forward for a position on the Committee, whether you’ve been with the club 50 years or 5 minutes. So long as you’re a paid up member (for 2018) you can be on the Committee.

The Committee positions are as follows (together with who is currently in that role):

If you’d like any further information about any of the roles, contact Paul who can send you a brief description of the role. Alternatively, contact the Committee member who is currently in the role you’re interested in. Any of us would be happy to tell you what’s involved.
