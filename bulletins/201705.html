April Weekend Meet

We’ve just had the April Hut Meet:

Dave, Simon, Ken, Mel, Rob and Kim went to Moel y Gest on the Saturday to get some climbing and some leading practice in. Whilst this madness was going on, Guy, Mike, Emma and Sarah went walking around Beddgelert. Cliff and Vanessa arrived just in time to have a beer or two before diving into Dave's chilli which could have quite easily fed the entire Club had everyone been there, and there would still have been leftovers!

On the Sunday, Craig, Vanessa, Rob, Sarah and Mike summited Craig Cwm Silyn whilst Simon and Dave completed ‘One Step in the Clouds’ and ‘Kestrel Cracks’. As it was a Bank Holiday Monday, Cliff, Mike and Vanessa stayed on and the plan was to complete ‘Arthur's Chair’…

Thanks to everyone who made it!
