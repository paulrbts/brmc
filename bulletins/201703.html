Hut Meets

There are now TWO hut meets arranged for March:

17-19 March: Hut Maintenance Weekend

We shall be having two Hut Maintenance Weekends a year; one at the start of the season and one at the end of the season for general maintenance. The Hut Sub-Committee plan to finish the hut works this year, but in the meantime we need to keep it maintained for all users.

If you have time to come up and spend time cleaning, painting, maintaining, not only will there be no hut fees, but there will be a free drink waiting for you at the Golden Fleece and the Club will be paying for the communal meal on the Saturday night.

Also, if we work hard enough and get the jobs done, there will be time to play on the crag or stretch our legs on the hills.

Contact Dave or Mike for further details.

24-26 March 2017: New Members' Weekend.

This is a great opportunity for new members to come up for a weekend at the hut, meet those of us who can't get to pub meets and also have an opportunity to have a go at activities you may not have have tried before (weather dependent). The focus is purely on the new members!

There are no hut fees payable for this weekend and there will be a communal meal on the Saturday evening. Contact Annelyse for further details.

BMC Club Roadshows

These events are to provide you with updates on the latest news, programmes and support from the BMC, and your chance to have a say on the future direction and content of club support.

The Roadshows will be replacing the Clubs Seminar for 2017 and will be taking place during February and March at four venues across the country to make them easier for more people to access.

  • Saturday 18 February BMC Offices, Manchester
  • Saturday 25 February YHA, London Lee Valley
  • Saturday 11 March Redpoint Climbing Centre, Worcester

More details are available at: www.thebmc.co.uk/clubs-roadshows-2017. If you do manage to get to one, we'd love to hear about it.
