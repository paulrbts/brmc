AGM 2016 & Old Documents

The 2016 AGM was very well attended and the minutes will be available soon for those of you who were unable to attend. Congratulations to Pete Redfern for winning the photo competition with his last minute entry. Pete's photo has taken pride of place on the Facebook page and you will see it on the membership cards which will be ready in the new year.

We were very lucky that some older members attended: Keith Lucas, Geoff Briscoe, Bob Reeve, Nigel Bunyan and Andy Spires. With them came not only a bundle of old documents from the 1970s through to the 1990s, but some amazing pictures also from that period. We can certainly learn a lot from that time, having looked at the Meets List, Newsletters and general activities the Club got up to back in the day.

However, it is becoming apparent that there may be more old documents out there and for the Club's 50th Anniversary this year it would be great to collate them all.

If anyone has any old Club documents they would be willing to pass on to the Committee or make us aware of, please let one of us know.

Communication

Feedback from members generally and at the AGM is that communication is challenging within the Club, despite all the avenues of communication available to us nowadays. This is one of the reasons for bringing back the Bulletin. Some members only receive e-mail, some are on e-mail and the forum and some all three, or a selection of the above.

Can we encourage everyone to register on the forum (which may have an overhaul in 2017) and ask that everything that is arranged is posted on both the forum and Facebook to ensure that it reaches as much of the membership as possible. Nigel's Facebook post on 29 December was the perfect example of this—thanks Nigel!

If you would like the Committee to e-mail out any information or include it in the newsletter, let us know!
